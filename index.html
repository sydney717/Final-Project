Acetone Detection

The presence of acetone in exhaled breath can be a sign of various medical conditions. These chemicals in vapor form can be detected by colorimetric indicators. This app allows you to submit a photo of the colorimetric sensors to determine the presence of acetone as well as the concentration.

THIS SHOULD NOT BE USED TO DIAGNOSE ANY MEDICAL CONDITION as it is still a prototype.
